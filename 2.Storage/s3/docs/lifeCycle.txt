LifeCycle Rules:
    -Set of rules t o move data between different tiers, to save storage cost
    -Example: General Purpose =>Infrequent Access =>Glacier
    -Transition actions:
        -It defines when objects are transitioned to another class.
    -Expiration actions:
        -Helps to configure objects to expire after certain time period
    -Moving to glacier is helpful to backup / long term retention
